A software company experimenting with future Technology

LiwaTec is a celebration of humanity and the wonderful things we can accomplish with design, technology, and innovation.

We focus on technology because we believe that technology does not just solve our problems. It also shapes who we are, how we work, how we live, and how we interact with the world around us.

Our goal with this website is to showcase our products and to honor the work of other people creating technology that changes our lives for the better.

Our mission is to create a world where technology is build on solid principles that are life-affirming, sustainable, and well designed for us.
